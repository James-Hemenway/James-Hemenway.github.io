About Me

Hey there! I'm James, a designer, programmer, and 3D artist from the PNW. Ever since I could remember I have been fascinated with creating new worlds for people to explore and crafting novel experiences. I have 6+ years of experience working with the Unity Engine and the .NET framework for game creation. I also have a strong understanding of the character creation workflow for video games. In my spare time, I like to garden, play video games, and every now and again, fail miserably at picking up a new instrument.

On this website, you can find some blogs on my workflow, some cool details on some projects I've worked on, and even some playable demos! If you have any questions or just want to get in contact, there's a form at the bottom of this page just for that.

Thanks for stopping by!
